About Me

Raised in the heritage town of Argao, I developed values of hard work, humility, and respect. I graduated as class valedictorian in both elementary and junior high, actively joining competitions and musical groups that took me across the Philippines.

In senior high, I pursued the STEM strand and graduated with high honors. I briefly studied Mechanical Engineering and Military Science before choosing Taiwan for its advanced education and industry — a decision driven by my desire to prepare for the future.

My Motivation

My goal is to support my family and grow through education. I believe in learning through experience, and Taiwan offers the perfect environment for both technical growth and cultural exchange.

Life in Taiwan

Working in a factory has taught me discipline and precision. At Shu-Te University, I’ve found a supportive academic space that fuels my passion for learning — from engineering to language and culture.

Looking Ahead

I aim to bring these lessons home to the Philippines, to inspire others and contribute to my community. Every challenge is a step toward becoming a better version of myself.
